Location details

We'll be celebrating at En Public in Griftpark. It's easy to reach from the city and around 15 minutes from Utrecht Centraal. The space gives us the best of both: a lovely terrace outside and a comfortable indoor area, so we'll be able to enjoy the evening whatever the weather.

What to wear

The overall style of the celebration is cocktail attire — elegant, relaxed, and festive, without being too formal. Think polished and occasion-ready, but not black tie.

For women
Cocktail dresses
Soft, elegant, celebration-ready
We'd love to see you in cocktail-style outfits, ideally dresses. Soft, elegant, and celebration-ready looks will fit the mood beautifully. Please avoid white and black.

If you'd like to keep the celebration going, we'd love to see you again the next day for a relaxed afternoon in the park. We'll meet at 16:00 in Wilhelminapark for a casual BBQ, fresh air, and a slower moment together after the wedding day.
